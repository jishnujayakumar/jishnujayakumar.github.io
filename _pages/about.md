My research is about robot learning from **few examples and from humans**. The standard recipe &mdash; massive labeled datasets or hours of teleoperation &mdash; isn&rsquo;t necessary; with a strong pretrained backbone underneath, three small signals often suffice: a handful of exemplar images, a single human demonstration video, or a few minutes of in-situ correction. My in-progress dissertation, *Learning from Humans and Few Examples for Embodied Robotics*, develops this idea across [FewSOL](https://irvlutd.github.io/FewSOL/){:target="_blank"} (a 336-object dataset for few-shot object learning), [Proto-CLIP](https://irvlutd.github.io/Proto-CLIP/){:target="_blank"} (vision-language few-shot classification), [iTeach](https://irvlutd.github.io/iTeach/){:target="_blank"} (in-situ adaptation of perception from human-flagged failures), and [HRT1](https://irvlutd.github.io/HRT1/){:target="_blank"} (one-shot mobile manipulation from a single human demonstration video &mdash; **98% grasp success and 92% task completion on 16 real-world tasks**).

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at the [University of Texas at Dallas](https://www.utdallas.edu){:target="_blank"} ([Erik Jonsson School of Engineering and Computer Science](https://engineering.utdallas.edu){:target="_blank"}), advised by [Dr. Yu Xiang](https://yuxng.github.io/){:target="_blank"} at the [Intelligent Robotics and Vision Lab](https://labs.utdallas.edu/irvl/){:target="_blank"} (IRVL). I earned my MSCS at the same school in 2022 before continuing on for the Ph.D.

Before UTD, I was a Research Intern at [NVIDIA](https://www.nvidia.com/en-in){:target="_blank"} Bengaluru and at [IIT Kharagpur](http://cse.iitkgp.ac.in/){:target="_blank"} ([CNeRG](https://cnerg-iitkgp.github.io/){:target="_blank"}) under [Dr. Saptarshi Ghosh](https://sites.google.com/site/saptarshighosh/){:target="_blank"}, and spent two formative years as a Research { Intern, Assistant, Associate } at [RBCCPS](https://cps.iisc.ac.in/){:target="_blank"}, [Indian Institute of Science](https://www.iisc.ac.in/){:target="_blank"}. I graduated with a B.Tech. from IIIT Vadodara in 2017, where I worked as a Research Assistant and completed my baccalaureate thesis with [Dr. Bhargab Chattopadhyay](https://vidwan.inflibnet.ac.in/profile/123829){:target="_blank"} and [Dr. Pradipta Ray](https://personal.utdallas.edu/~pradiptaray/){:target="_blank"} on a sequential approach for noninferiority and equivalence testing of linear contrasts under cost constraints.

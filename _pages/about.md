I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate at the [University of Texas at Dallas](https://www.utdallas.edu){:target="_blank"} (graduating 2027), advised by [Dr. Yu Xiang](https://yuxng.github.io/){:target="_blank"} at the [Intelligent Robotics and Vision Lab (IRVL)](https://labs.utdallas.edu/irvl/){:target="_blank"}. My research builds **foundation models for embodied perception and robot manipulation** &mdash; few-shot and multimodal learning frameworks that let robots understand and adapt to the physical world from vision, language, and human demonstrations.

My work has appeared at **ICRA** and **IROS** with **100+ citations**, and was recognized with the **Best Poster Award at AI Days 2026** and the **Louis Beecherl Jr. Graduate Fellowship** (2026&ndash;2027). Recent projects: [HRT1](https://irvlutd.github.io/HRT1/){:target="_blank"} (one-shot human-to-robot trajectory transfer), [Proto-CLIP](https://irvlutd.github.io/Proto-CLIP/){:target="_blank"} (vision-language few-shot learning), [iTeach](https://irvlutd.github.io/iTeach/){:target="_blank"} (interactive teaching via mixed reality), [NIDS-Net](https://irvlutd.github.io/NIDSNet/){:target="_blank"} (novel instance detection), and the [FewSOL](https://irvlutd.github.io/FewSOL/){:target="_blank"} dataset. I currently collaborate with the [RAI Institute](https://rai-inst.com/){:target="_blank"} on dynamic manipulation primitives &mdash; work featured in [IEEE Spectrum](https://spectrum.ieee.org/video-friday-baseball-robot){:target="_blank"}.

Before UTD, I was a Research Intern at [NVIDIA](https://www.nvidia.com/en-in){:target="_blank"} (Bengaluru) and at [IIT Kharagpur](http://cse.iitkgp.ac.in/){:target="_blank"} ([CNeRG](https://cnerg-iitkgp.github.io/){:target="_blank"}) under [Dr. Saptarshi Ghosh](https://sites.google.com/site/saptarshighosh/){:target="_blank"}, and spent two years at the [Indian Institute of Science](https://www.iisc.ac.in/){:target="_blank"} ([RBCCPS](https://cps.iisc.ac.in/){:target="_blank"}). I hold a B.Tech. from IIIT Vadodara (2017).

<strong style="color:#f30000">I am always open to mentoring motivated MS and undergraduate students interested in robot/deep learning research. If you have strong programming skills and enthusiasm for research, feel free to reach out via email.</strong>
